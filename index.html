    Welcome to Sarnaik Farms
    Empowering Farmers, Enriching Our Future

    Welcome to Sarnaik Farms, where we are passionately committed to supporting farmers throughout every stage of their agricultural journey. Our comprehensive approach includes providing high-quality seeds, advanced farming techniques, and modern agricultural tools, ensuring that farmers are well-equipped to thrive from planting to harvest. We emphasize sustainable practices such as crop diversification, soil health improvement, and water conservation, aiming to boost productivity while protecting the environment. In addition to our agricultural initiatives, we also extend our commitment to our dairy operations, where we work closely with local farmers, ensuring fair trade practices and a reliable collection network.

    At Sarnaik Farms, our naturally made dairy products, including fresh dahi, creamy shrikhand, invigorating lassi, and sweet pedha, reflect our dedication to quality and purity. By fostering innovation and sustainability, we aim to promote the well-being of both farmers and consumers, offering products that are both delicious and nourishing.

    About Us

    At Sarnaik Farms, our passion lies in crafting dairy products that blend natural goodness with exceptional flavor. By harmonizing innovative farming techniques with time-honored traditions, we ensure each product embodies our unwavering commitment to quality. Our diverse range of premium dairy items—milk, lassi, dahi, paneer, shrikhand, matha, and our signature Keshav Nagar Pedha—are crafted from the finest natural ingredients to deliver freshness and taste that exceed expectations. Our dedication to quality extends beyond just our products. We are equally committed to supporting the backbone of our industry—our farmers. From the initial planning stages through to harvesting and marketing, Sarnaik Farms provides farmers with top-notch seeds, cutting-edge tools, and expert guidance. Our approach emphasizes sustainable practices that boost yields while safeguarding the environment.

    Connecting farmers to fair markets is central to our mission. We create transparent, supportive ecosystems that empower farmers with the knowledge and resources they need to succeed. By integrating education on best practices and emerging agricultural trends, we help farmers grow with confidence and contribute to a resilient agricultural community. At Sarnaik Farms, we are dedicated to nurturing a vibrant agricultural sector and offering dairy products that reflect our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the well-being of our farmers. Together, we are cultivating a future where every farmer thrives, and every product is a testament to our dedication to excellence.

    Sarnaik Farm Dairy Products

    At Sarnaik Farms, we take pride in offering high-quality, naturally made dairy products that reflect traditional craftsmanship. Our creamy dahi, a staple in Indian households, is cherished for its rich texture and probiotic benefits. Our shrikhand is a smooth, sweet yogurt-based dessert, perfect for satisfying sweet cravings with natural ingredients. We also offer refreshing lassi, made with the finest ingredients, ideal for cooling down on hot days. Our sweet pedha, a traditional Indian confection, is crafted using time-honored methods for a perfect balance of sweetness and texture.

    We prioritize quality and purity, ensuring all our products are free from artificial additives and preservatives. This commitment preserves authentic flavors and maintains the nutritional value of our dairy products. Choosing Sarnaik Farms means selecting products made with love and respect for nature's bounty. Our dairy products are rich in essential nutrients like calcium, protein, and probiotics, making them a healthy addition to your diet. By supporting Sarnaik Farms, you enjoy premium dairy products and promote sustainable farming practices.

    Our Licences and Certifications

    Our dairy products, including dahi, shrikhand, lassi, and pedha, are proudly certified by FSSAI. This prestigious certification guarantees that our products adhere to the most stringent standards of quality and safety.

    The FSSAI certification process involves rigorous testing and compliance with the best practices in food safety, ensuring that every product we offer is fresh, hygienic, and nutritious. Our commitment extends beyond just meeting these standards; we aim to exceed them by continually improving our processes and maintaining transparency with our customers.

    By choosing our dairy products, you are assured of receiving high-quality, authentic items that contribute to a healthy lifestyle. We take pride in our dedication to excellence and the trust our customers place in us. Thank you for being a part of our journey toward delivering superior dairy products.

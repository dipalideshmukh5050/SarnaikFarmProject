    Who Are We?

    Innovative Farming, Superior Dairy

    At Sarnaik Farms, excellence is our tradition. Renowned for our commitment to superior farming practices and dairy products, we stand as a beacon of quality and innovation in the industry. Our farm is a testament to the harmonious balance between nature and modern agricultural techniques, ensuring that every product we deliver is of the highest caliber.

    Our dedication to farming excellence is reflected in every aspect of our operations. We embrace sustainable farming methods that enrich the soil, conserve resources, and enhance the quality of our produce. By integrating cutting-edge technology with time-honored practices, we continuously push the boundaries of agricultural innovation.

    At Sarnaik Dairy, our passion for dairy products shines through. From farm-fresh milk to our premium range of dairy delights including dahi, shrikhand, lassi, and pedha, each product is crafted with meticulous care and the finest ingredients. Our dairy is celebrated for its exceptional freshness and rich flavor, embodying the essence of our farm's dedication to quality.

    At Sarnaik Farms, we craft pure, naturally made dairy products with a focus on quality. Our FSSAI certification underscores our commitment to freshness and excellence.

    Sarnaik Farms: Pioneering Agriculture and Dairy Solutions for a Brighter Future

    At Sarnaik Farms, "Our focus is to make the farmers of the district financially capable and progressive." We believe in fostering growth and sustainability within the agricultural community. By providing the necessary resources and support, we aim to uplift the livelihoods of farmers, ensuring their continued success for generations to come. Sarnaik Dairy, established in 2020, plays a crucial role in this mission. We collect milk from 35 villages, involving 400 milk-producing farmers, including 110 women. Our dairy products, ranging from dahi, shrikhand, paneer, pedha, and ghee to lassi, mattha, and tak, are processed and distributed through our two sell outlets.

    Farming

    Empowering Farmers & Promoting Sustainable Agriculture

    At Sarnaik Farms, we are deeply committed to supporting farmers through every stage of the agricultural journey. From seed selection and soil preparation to harvest and distribution, we are with our farmers at every step, providing them with the tools, knowledge, and support they need to cultivate high-quality crops. We believe in sustainable farming practices that not only improve yields but also protect the environment, ensuring a better future for generations to come. Together, we are dedicated to making agriculture more efficient, sustainable, and prosperous for all.

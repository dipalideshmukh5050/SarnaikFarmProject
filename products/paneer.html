    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: How long does the paneer stay fresh?

    A: The paneer remains fresh for up to 10 days if stored in a cool, dry place.

    Q: Can I freeze the paneer?

    A: Yes, you can freeze the paneer for up to 3 months. Make sure it is properly sealed to avoid freezer burn.

    Q: Is the paneer suitable for vegetarians?

    A: Yes, our paneer is suitable for vegetarians as it does not contain any animal products.
